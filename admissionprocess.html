Admission Details

Admission process

Newly admitted pupil of St Edward's

Basically, the admission process is as simple as possible. As a parent, if you want to admit your child to the St Edward's Primary School,all that you have to do is come to the school with your child and register your kid. In order to adhere with the government agenda for free and quality education, the admission process is free. For more information you can contact the school's authorities.

Admission Authority

Usually, the admission process is done by the head teachers. Our school is made up of two shift (afternoon shift and morning shift) Therefore, there are two head teachers one for morning shift and another for afternoon shift. For more information contact the school's head teachers.

When to admit your child to St Edward's Primary School

A school can best be judge by it performances in every public examination. Having browsed through all the above-mentions link one would come to realize that St Edward's Primary is the appropriate school for your child.

Who to represent pupil's in the admission process.

Parents are the main representatives that the school authorities acknowledge for the admission of their kids to St Edward's Primary School.Even though some other relatives like pupil's sister, cousin, brother etc are allowed only if their is a proof that the pupil in question is under his or her auspices or has been chosen by the pupil's biological parents as representative. For more advice on whom to represent your children for admission to St Edward's Primary School please contact school authorities.
