Uniform Order

At St Edward's Primary school we believe that compliance with school uniform is important to the following reasons:

  • It helps reduce social conflict based on fashion within the school.
  • It enhances school loyalty and build community spirit.
  • It improves effective learning community where pupils
    are not distracted by trend of fashion.
  • It sets the standard by which we set all our ethics and principles.
