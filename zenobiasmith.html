Biography


Mrs Zenobia Smith

Mrs. Smith graduated in 2014 from the reknown Freetown Teacher's College
with a Teacher's Certificate (T.C) and Higher Teacher's Certificate (HTC).
She commenced her teaching career at FAWE Kroo Bay Community from 2004-2013.
She started teaching in St Edward's Primary School in September 2013.
She is the current teacher for class 5 one. Mrs Smith has had Seven Years experience in teaching.
She has maintained a very cordial relationship with her pupils.
"St Edward's Primary School is a grade A school" said Mrs Smith .
