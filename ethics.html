Pupil's Privacy Policy

Synopsis

Through out this document, wherever the term "we", "school", "us" or "ours" are used this refers to St Edward's Primary School and wherever "you" or "your" are used this refers to a pupil.


Why are you reading this note?

As your school of choice, we need to make use of certain information we collect from you and this is for countable reasons. This document gives you a clear insight of the information we collect about you and what we can do with them. It is of grave importance to us that your informations remain secret within the school's administration but not with a third party. Below is a brief explanation of how your informations are kept secret.

Should you want to know anything about the information we collect about you, please contact the school's administration or ask your teacher or speak to you parents or guardians to do an enquiries on your behalf. The school is ready and willing to answer to all your questions.

Written by Head Teacher

Policy Statement

During your time with us, we will make use of the information we gather about you for certain number of reasons. Information we keep in relation to you is known as "personal data". This includes the information we collect from you directly and the one we collect from other people and organizations. We may also hold on to your personal data for so long even if you have left the school. Whatever we do to your personal data is known as processing. This paper sets out what type of information we collect about you and why we process them, who we share them with and your right in relation to your personal data processed by us.
