Biography


Mrs Sallay Kebbie

Mrs. Kebbie is one of the last remaininig teachers that
have been teaching in St Edward's Primary School for over 20 years.
She was born on 30th December 1962 at Pujehun District.
She graduated in 1999 from the Freetown Teacher's College.
She earned her T.C general in 1999 and started teaching in St Edward's Primary School in 1989.
Mrs Kebbie has had Seven Years experience in teaching.
She maintains a very cordial relationship with her pupils.
